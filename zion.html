Zion National Park

Springdale, Utah

About the Park

Zion National Park is located in southwestern Utah and is famous for its towering red and white sandstone cliffs. The Virgin River carved the stunning Zion Canyon over millions of years creating one of the most breathtaking landscapes in the world.

Best Hikes

Angels Landing is one of the most famous hikes in the US — a 5 mile round trip trail with chains bolted into the rock to help you reach the summit. A permit is required.

The Narrows is a unique hike through the Virgin River itself, walking through slot canyons with walls rising 1000 feet above you. Best done in summer when water levels are lower.

Emerald Pools Trail is a family friendly 3 mile hike leading to a series of beautiful pools and waterfalls tucked into the canyon walls.
